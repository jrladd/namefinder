
 
 
 TO all the Officers of the Army, Colonells, Lieutenant-Colonells, Serjeant-Majors, Captaines, other Officers and Souldiers of Horse and Foot, and all others whom these Lawes and Ordinances shall concerne. 
 VVHich  Lawes  and  Ordinances  hereby published, all the said Persons respectively and severally, are Required and Commanded to observe and keepe, on the Paines and Penalties therein expressed.
