
 
 
 TO HIS HONOVRED FRIENDS, Sr. G. C. AND HIS VERTUOUS LADIE,  A. 
 Sir, Madam, 
 Y Ou have beene informed of my Loyalty, and beleeve it; I have felt your Charity, and acknowledge it: That the World may say, I dyed a loyall Subject, and a thankefull Servant, I have left these lines as a
 
testimony of both, from him that was while he lived, 
 Sir, Madam, 
 Your Beads-man,  T. S.
